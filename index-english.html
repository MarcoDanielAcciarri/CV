About me

I am a 25-year-old physicist with a background in aeronautical engineering.
I am about to complete a master in Physics at Instituto Balseiro.
I have knowledge of numerical simulation and experience in developing codes for fluids and plasmas simulation.
I have extensive experience of laboratory work in design and manufacturing of new materials using different techniques (powder metallurgy, casting, SOL-GEL) and characterization using RX, EDS, LEED, AES, mechanical tests, and calorimetry, among others. I have published two papers related to the topic and participated as an expositor in five different conferences presenting new materials and their properties.
I have basic knowledge of Machine Learning and Deep Learning, development of neural network models using Keras and Tensorflow.
In addition, I have done courses and training in high performance computing and parallel programming through CUDA-C, PyCuda, Cupy.
